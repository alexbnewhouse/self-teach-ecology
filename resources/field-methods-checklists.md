# Field Methods Checklists

Use these checklists for restoration and rewilding field practice.

## Pre-Field Checklist

- Site access permissions and safety plan confirmed
- Objectives translated into measurable indicators
- Sampling design selected (quadrat, transect, plot)
- Datasheets or digital forms prepared
- GPS, camera, and backups verified

## Vegetation Survey Checklist

- Plot/transect locations georeferenced
- Species composition recorded consistently
- Cover/abundance method documented
- Invasive species presence logged
- Photos captured from fixed points

## Soil and Habitat Condition Checklist

- Soil sampling depth and protocol standardized
- Evidence of erosion/compaction documented
- Hydrology and moisture context recorded
- Disturbance indicators logged (fire, grazing, trampling)

## Monitoring and Adaptive Management Checklist

- Baseline established and timestamped
- Recovery indicators linked to targets
- Decision thresholds defined
- Monitoring frequency calendar set
- Triggered response actions pre-defined

## Data Management Checklist

- Metadata complete (who, where, when, method)
- File naming convention applied
- Versioned data archive created
- Data license and reuse constraints noted
