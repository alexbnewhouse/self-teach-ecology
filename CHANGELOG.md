# Changelog

All notable changes to this curriculum are documented here.

## [1.3.0] - 2026-03-02

### Added

- Political Ecology specialization track (`curriculum/tracks/political-ecology.md`) with four units: Canon & Intellectual History, Conservation as Politics, Political Ecology of Restoration, Quantitative Political Ecology.
- ML/AI for Conservation pathway (Pathway F) in Computational Ecology track: camera trap ML, satellite deep learning, NLP for conservation policy, bioacoustics, independent project.
- Career Transition Guide (`resources/career-transition-guide.md`): three career trajectories, organizations, conferences, funding, credibility building, PSCI PhD positioning.
- PSCI-to-Ecology Concept Bridge (`resources/psci-ecology-bridge.md`): maps 8 conceptual domains from political science / complex systems onto ecological equivalents.
- Auto-graded exercise bank for Political Ecology track.

### Changed

- README dashboard updated with Political Ecology track in Tier 2 table, new recommended bundle (PSCI → Data/ML Ecologist), updated hour budget (850–1,316), version bumped to 1.3.0.
- Electives index (`curriculum/09-electives.md`) updated: Political Ecology as priority track #4, new Bundle B.
- PROGRESS.md expanded with Tier 4 gates, ML/AI and political ecology artifact checklists, ecological credibility milestones, career reflection prompts.
- Competency framework Domain 3 now references Political Ecology track.
- Benchmark mapping updated with political ecology and ML/AI coverage notes.
- Comprehensive exam blueprint expanded with political ecology prompt option.
- Reading lists updated with political ecology and conservation ML references.
- Exercise index updated with Political Ecology and Pathway F exercises.
