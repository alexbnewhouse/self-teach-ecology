## ðŸ’» Exercises & Activities

### Week 1-2: Population Ecology

**Exercise 1.1: Population Growth Models**
- Implement exponential and logistic growth models in R or Python
- Compare predictions with real data (e.g., bacterial culture, wildlife populations)
- Explore how parameters (r, K) affect population trajectories
- **Deliverable:** Annotated code with visualizations

**Exercise 1.2: Life History Analysis**
- Compare life history strategies of 5 different species (r vs. K selection)
- Create life tables and survivorship curves from published data
- **Deliverable:** Report with comparative analysis and graphs

### Week 3-4: Community Ecology

**Exercise 2.1: Species Interaction Matrix**
- Analyze a food web from a local ecosystem
- Calculate connectance, interaction strength, and network properties
- Predict effects of species removal (keystone species concept)
- **Deliverable:** Network diagram and analysis report

**Exercise 2.2: Biodiversity Assessment**
- Calculate diversity indices (Shannon, Simpson) from species count data
- Compare diversity across different habitats or time periods
- Discuss factors affecting biodiversity patterns
- **Deliverable:** Statistical analysis with interpretation

### Week 5-6: Ecosystem Ecology

**Exercise 3.1: Energy Flow Analysis**
- Diagram energy flow through trophic levels in an ecosystem
- Calculate ecological efficiencies between trophic levels
- Compare terrestrial vs. aquatic systems
- **Deliverable:** Energy flow diagram with calculations

**Exercise 3.2: Nutrient Cycling**
- Map a complete nutrient cycle (carbon, nitrogen, or phosphorus)
- Identify human impacts on the cycle
- Propose management strategies to restore natural cycling
- **Deliverable:** Illustrated cycle diagram with annotations

### Week 7-8: Synthesis & Application

**Project 1: Case Study Analysis**
Choose a real conservation challenge and apply ecological principles:
- Describe the ecological system and conservation issue
- Apply population, community, and ecosystem concepts to understand the problem
- Propose evidence-based interventions
- **Deliverable:** 2000-word case study report
