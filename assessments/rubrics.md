# Assessment Rubrics

Scoring scale used across curriculum deliverables:

- 1 = Developing
- 2 = Proficient
- 3 = Advanced

---

## Rubric A: Reading Synthesis Essays

| Criterion | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Argument clarity | Mostly descriptive | Clear thesis and structure | Original, compelling synthesis |
| Evidence use | Sparse or mismatched | Appropriate citations and interpretation | Integrates evidence critically across sources |
| Conceptual rigor | Errors or omissions | Accurate concept use | Deep conceptual integration and critique |

## Rubric B: Computational Notebooks

| Criterion | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reproducibility | Missing steps/environment details | Re-runnable with minor friction | Fully reproducible with environment and data provenance |
| Methods fit | Methods weakly matched to question | Methods are appropriate and justified | Strong method selection with alternative checks |
| Interpretation | Output reported with little ecology | Ecological interpretation is clear | Interpretation is nuanced with uncertainty limits |

## Rubric C: Policy and Governance Analyses

| Criterion | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Framework use | Mentions frameworks only | Applies frameworks coherently | Uses frameworks comparatively with critical insight |
| Ecological grounding | Limited ecological content | Ecological dimensions are integrated | Strong social-ecological synthesis with trade-offs |
| Practicality | Generic recommendations | Actionable recommendations | Sequenced, feasible strategy with evaluation logic |

## Rubric D: Capstone Quality Gate

| Criterion | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Research design | Unclear question or weak design | Clear, relevant question and coherent design | Novel, impactful design with strong validity logic |
| Analysis depth | Basic or incomplete analysis | Competent and correctly interpreted analysis | Robust, transparent, and comparative analysis |
| Scholarly quality | Limited synthesis and uneven writing | Well-structured, evidence-based writing | Publication-ready writing and contribution framing |

Completion target:

- Score at least 2 on every criterion.
- Score at least 3 on three criteria for capstone readiness.
