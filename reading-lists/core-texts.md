## How to Access Paywalled Materials

- **University library**: If you have any university affiliation (even alumni access), check library databases
- **Interlibrary loan**: Many public libraries offer ILL services
- **Author requests**: Email authors directly — most are happy to share PDFs
- **ResearchGate / Academia.edu**: Authors often post preprints
- **Preprint servers**: bioRxiv, EarthArXiv, SocArXiv
- **Open access versions**: Check Google Scholar for free versions
- **Textbook editions**: Older editions of textbooks are often much cheaper and nearly as good
