# Key Journal Articles

A curated list of seminal and contemporary journal articles organized by topic. These are the papers that have shaped the field — reading them will give you both foundational knowledge and the ability to engage with current debates.

**Citation format**: APA 7th edition

## Reading Strategy

1. **Start with reviews and syntheses** (#5, #13, #22, #45) before diving into specific empirical studies
2. **Read foundational papers first** within each topic (#1, #6, #10, #18, #33, #40)
3. **Use citation chains**: when a paper cites something interesting, follow it
4. **Keep an annotated bibliography** as you read — this will become invaluable for your capstone
5. **Read critically**: What is the evidence? What are the assumptions? What's missing?
