# self-teach-ecology

## Suggested modules

### Hyperlocal rewilding
- Native plant gardening for frontyards and backyards
- Site assessment for soil, sunlight, and water in small urban/suburban plots
- Pollinator habitat design with local keystone species
- Seasonal maintenance plans for low-input, biodiversity-supporting home landscapes

### Systems thinking and complexity
- Systems thinking fundamentals for ecology learners
- Stocks, flows, and feedback loops in social-ecological systems
- Leverage points and intervention design (Donella Meadows and related frameworks)
- Resilience, emergence, and adaptive cycles in complex ecosystems
